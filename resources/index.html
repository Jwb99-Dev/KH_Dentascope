Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often from dental practices considering a microscope.

Do I need a room assessment before purchasing a dental microscope?

A room assessment is highly recommended. Ceiling height, surgery layout and your preferred working position all affect which mount type and model will work best for your environment. We carry out assessments as part of our free, no-obligation service.

Which microscope brands do you supply?

We currently supply Alltion UK, KAPS and Xpedent — three of the most respected names in dental and surgical microscopy. We can advise on the strengths of each brand relative to your clinical requirements and budget.

Do you offer finance or payment plans?

Please get in touch to discuss financing options. We'll do our best to find a solution that works for your practice.

Can you install a microscope in an existing surgery?

Yes — the majority of our installations are into existing surgeries. We assess your room in advance and advise on any preparation required. Installations are typically completed in a single day.

Do you provide training after installation?

Absolutely. Comprehensive training is included as part of our installation service, and we provide ongoing support as you develop your clinical technique with the microscope.

Do you work with hospitals and universities?

Yes — we have extensive experience working in NHS and hospital environments as well as universities and dental training centres. We're happy to assist with procurement processes and multi-unit installations.
