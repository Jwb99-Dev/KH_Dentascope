Who We Are

Built on Experience. Driven by Quality.

KH Dentascope UK was originally built around Alltion microscopes, but has grown into a multi-brand dental microscopy specialist — supplying, installing and supporting equipment from Alltion UK, KAPS and Xpedent across the entire United Kingdom.

With over 25 years of experience in medical and dental sales — and extensive time working within NHS and hospital environments — the knowledge we bring to every enquiry is hard to match. We're not a corporate entity with a script. We're specialists who genuinely care about finding the right solution for your practice.

The KH Dentascope brand exists to provide exactly that: a more personal, flexible and expert alternative to larger corporate suppliers.
